# Claude Design prompt — Quinta logo + app icon

> Paste into Claude Design.

---

**Design a logo + app icon for "Quinta".**

Quinta is a modern music-theory app — an interactive **circle of fifths** and
chord-progression builder for songwriters and producers.

**Core concept:** a circle of fifths that **doubles as the letter "Q"**. The
circular ring is the body of the Q; a short, confident tail breaks the ring at
the lower-right to complete the Q. Iconic, geometric, and instantly readable even
at 24px.

**Musical layer (subtle — pick the ONE that reads cleanest at small sizes):**
- the 12 positions of the circle of fifths as 12 ticks/dots around the ring, or
- the 12-point star (dodecagram) you get when you connect the fifths, nested
  inside the ring, or
- the Q's tail styled as a musical note stem.
Don't overload it — one musical idea, executed cleanly.

**Style:** bold, modern, confident, flat and geometric with crisp edges. Premium,
not childish, not corporate. Must work in a single flat color, in negative space,
and as a tiny favicon.

**Color:** deep near-black background (#0a0a0b) with a **saturated electric yellow**
mark — high contrast, energetic. You may explore a subtle warm-yellow gradient for
depth, but it has to survive as flat yellow-on-black.

**Deliverables:**
- App icon, 1024×1024, balanced for a rounded-square mask, the Q centered.
- Horizontal wordmark: the Q mark + "Quinta" in clean, slightly characterful type.
- Variants: yellow-on-black **and** black-on-yellow, plus a 1-color monochrome.
- Show it tested at 48px and 24px.

**Vibe:** harmonic · electric · clean · a musician's tool.

Give me 4–6 distinct concept directions before refining.
