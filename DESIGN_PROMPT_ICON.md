# Prompt para Claude Design — App icon (Easy Fifth Circle)

*(Pegar tal cual en Claude Design. En inglés porque rinde mejor con la herramienta.)*

---

You are an app-icon designer. Design **app icon concepts** for **Easy Fifth Circle**, a
music app: a living circle of fifths where you pick a key, build chord progressions and
hear them. I do NOT like the previous icon directions — start fresh.

## The look I want
- **Modern iOS 26 "Liquid Glass" aesthetic**: layered translucent glass, real depth,
  soft specular highlights and inner light, a glossy refined squircle. Premium, tactile,
  a little dimensional — not flat.
- **Motif**: a **circle of fifths** as the hero — a clean ring with subtle nodes/pips
  around it — paired with **one musical element** (a single note / a tonic dot / a tasteful
  treble-clef curve). Keep it **simple and instantly readable**, even at 40px.
- **Background**: **saturated, beautiful colour** — a rich vibrant gradient that glows
  behind the glass. Bold but elegant, not neon-garish.
- **Feel**: creative, pretty, simple. One strong idea per icon, not busy.

## Colour
Free to explore, but the brand sits on an orange accent `#e8441a / #ff7048` and a plasma
palette you can pull saturated gradients from:
- Cosmos `#ff8a3d · #ff2e63 · #7b2ff7`
- Aurora `#00e0b8 · #2a7bff · #9b3cff`
- Dusk `#ff5fa2 · #a64bff · #ffb347`
- Amber `#ffd24a · #ff7a1a · #ff2e6e`
The ring/glyph can be white or a glass highlight; one node can be the orange accent.

## Rules (Apple icon spec)
- **No text**, no words.
- **Full-bleed squircle**, no transparency in the icon itself (the OS masks the corners).
- **Legible at 40px** — thick enough strokes/shapes that nothing disappears when small.
- Looks good on both light and dark home screens / any wallpaper.
- Master at **1024×1024**.

## Deliverables
- **5–6 distinct concepts**, each its own direction (e.g. orbit-ring, fifth-sweep arc,
  note-in-circle, glass-disc with pips, clef-meets-ring…).
- Show **each at 1024 / 180 / 120 / 60 / 40 px** so I can judge small-size legibility.
- A short note on which you'd recommend and why.
- Bonus: one mock of the recommended icon on a **dark and a light home screen**.

Make it feel like an icon I'd be proud to ship on the App Store.
